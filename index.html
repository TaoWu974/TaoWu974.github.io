About me

I'm a PhD student in Electronic Engineering with James Watt School of Engineering, University of Glasgow, Scotland, UK, under supervision of Prof. Bo Liu. I obtained a dual BEng degree in Electronic Engineering from University of Glasgow and UESTC and an MS in Computer Science from Georgia Institute of Technology.

My research focuses on designing radio frequency front-end components efficiently with optimization, (AI) surrogate model assisted optimization, machine learning and large language model (LLM).
